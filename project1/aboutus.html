ABOUT US

Victoria Sports Foundation Is An Initiative Of Sports With Over 17 Years Of Experience In Building Strength,Coordination,Confidence And Ground Work For A Healthy Lifestyle Among Young Kids Victoria Sports Promotes Physical Fitness Into The Everyday Routine Of The Kids. The Foundation Is Focused On Developing A Healthier and Filter Generation By Introducing A Structure Sports And Physical Education In School (P.E) Curriculum In School.

Victoria Sports Club Provides All Sport Training And Sports Event Like Dance, Music, Yoga,Zumba,Football,Cricket,Kabaddi,Volleyball,Basketball,Archery Martial Arts, Outdoor Fitness(Male & Female), Skating, Mallakhamb, Gymnastic, Kids Fitness And Adventure/Summer/Commando Training Camp In The Age Group Of 3 Years Above.
